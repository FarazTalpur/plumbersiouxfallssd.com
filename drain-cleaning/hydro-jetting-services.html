The Power of Hydro Jetting

Hydro jetting is a highly effective method for clearing stubborn clogs and buildup in your pipes. It uses high-pressure water jets to break apart and flush away debris, grease, and even tree roots that can cause significant plumbing issues.

Benefits of Hydro Jetting

  • Comprehensive Cleaning : Unlike traditional snaking or chemical solutions, hydro jetting cleans the entire pipe, not just unclogging it.
  • Eco-Friendly : This method uses pure water instead of harsh chemicals, making it an environmentally conscious choice.
  • Preventative Maintenance : Regular hydro jetting can prevent future blockages and extend the lifespan of your plumbing system.

Frequently Asked Questions

Hydro jetting is a cleaning method that uses high-pressure water streams to remove blockages, roots, and buildup in plumbing pipes, restoring optimal flow and preventing future clogs.

Unlike traditional snaking, which physically breaks up clogs with an auger, hydro jetting uses high-pressure water to thoroughly clean pipe interiors, removing debris and buildup that snaking might leave behind.

Hydro jetting is generally safe for most pipes, but older or more fragile pipes may require inspection to ensure they can withstand the pressure. Professional assessment is recommended before proceeding.

Yes, hydro jetting is effective at cutting through tree roots that have infiltrated pipes. It can restore flow and clear obstructions, but in severe cases, recurring root issues may require additional solutions.

The frequency of hydro jetting depends on the condition of the pipes and the volume of usage. For most residential properties, once every 18-24 months is sufficient, but high-use or commercial properties may benefit from more frequent service.
